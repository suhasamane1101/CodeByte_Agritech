Types of Insurance

Crop Insurance losses due to natural calamities like floods, droughts, hailstorms, pests, and diseases. It's mandatory for farmers who avail loan from banks for notified crops.
Losses due to deviations from weather parameters like rainfall, temperature, and wind speed..
Livestock Insurance Covers death due to natural causes, accidents, diseases, and theft. Different breeds and ages have separate options. Protects against mortality due to diseases, predators, and natural calamities.death due to natural causes, accidents, and certain diseases for cattle, buffaloes, and sheep. Premiums are subsidized by the government, making it affordable for farmers.
Personal Insurance comprehensive scheme combines crop insurance, livestock insurance, personal accident insurance, and hut insurance. Offers accidental death cover and a personal loan facility. Affordable plans covering specific risks like failed wells, pump-set breakdown, and cycle rickshaw damage
